Singersongwriter Mateo Marín Calderón released on December 2022 the debut album of his solo project, a transatlantic collaboration in which Latin American song and poetry meet with a modern perspective anew.

The artist’s first album is a collection of 8 poems set to music and a remix. It is the product of long-distance collaboration between musicians, poets and producers in different Latin American and European countries. Its conception began in 2021 and developed over 2 years, parallel to the pandemic. The result was peaceful, meditative, often nostalgic and reflective songs, accompanied by sometimes sweet, other colorful and occasionally mysterious sound textures, in different acoustic and semi-acoustic sets.
